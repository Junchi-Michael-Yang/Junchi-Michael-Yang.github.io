Junchi Yang

 

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at ETH Zurich, advised by Niao He. I also work closely with Negar Kiyavash. My research interest lies in optimization and its applications in machine learning. Before that, I received master's degree in Industrial Engineering from University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I received bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics and Economics from UCLA in 2017.
