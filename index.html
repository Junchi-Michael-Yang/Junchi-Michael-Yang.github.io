Junchi Yang

 

I am a final-year PhD student in Computer Science at ETH Zurich, advised by Niao He. I also work closely with Negar Kiyavash. My research interest lies in optimization and its applications in machine learning. Prior to this, I received a master's degree in Industrial Engineering from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I received a bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics and Economics from UCLA in 2017.
